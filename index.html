About Cinesamples

Founded in Los Angeles in 2007, Cinesamples has grown to become an industry leader in the development of top virtual instruments and sample libraries.

We are passionate about the sampling process, and you can hear our products in many films, TV shows, and video games. Our libraries are recorded at legendary scoring stages including MGM and the Alfred Newman Stage at Fox, and mixed by Oscar-nominated engineers.

Now, through our partnership with Musio, we're making our entire catalog more accessible than ever before.
